Absolute Date from Date


Instructions

The absolute date is the date expressed as the number of days since 31-Dec-1600. To calculate the absolute date from the month, day, and year, perform these steps:

  1. Enter the desired date in the Month, Day, and Year fields.
  2. Click the Calculate button.

The page displays the absolute date for the specified month, day, and year.
