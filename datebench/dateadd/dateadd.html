Instructions

To calculate the date resulting from adding or subtracting a number of days from another date, perform these steps:

  1. Enter the desired date in the Month, Day, and Year fields.
  2. Enter the number of days in the Days field. The value can be negative, positive, or zero.
  3. Click the Calculate button.

The page displays the date resulting from adding the value of days to the original date.
