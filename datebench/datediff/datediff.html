Instructions

To calculate the difference between two dates, perform these steps:

  1. Enter a date in First Date fields.
  2. Enter the date to be subtracted from the first date in the Minus Date fields.
  3. Click the Calculate button.

The page will display the number of days between the first date and the second date. The number can be positive, negative, or zero.
